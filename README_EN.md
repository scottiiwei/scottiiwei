## About me

I am interested in how **AI can improve real business workflows**, especially in e-commerce, content operations, customer communication, instant messaging, and intelligent automation.

I studied Information Technology and Network Security in Australia, with academic experience at **Queensland University of Technology** and **Griffith University**. Over the past 5 years, I have worked in **content operations for e-commerce platforms**. I am currently involved in an AI integration project for an e-commerce platform's instant messaging product, focusing on **AI training and workflow improvement**.

I enjoy learning how modern AI systems are trained, evaluated, and applied inside real products. I also follow the US stock market and like studying valuable technology companies from a long-term perspective.

Outside work, I enjoy marathon running and swimming. I once went from 85kg to 65kg, losing 20kg. That experience taught me how much long-term thinking, discipline, and consistent action can change a person.

I also love traveling and experiencing different cultures, cities, languages, and ways of living. The scenery does not always have to be perfect. Sometimes what matters is one brief moment: we were there, standing in that place, together with someone we love. My dream is to travel around the world.
